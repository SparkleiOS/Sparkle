Can't find a device to cast?
Check the following tips
1.
When the TV/box is turned on, it must be connected to the same Wi-Fi as the mobile phone.
2.
Make sure your TV/box supports projection.
If you are not clear, consult the equipment manufacturer or the customer service of the purchase channel.
3.
Verify that the screen projection software installed on your TV/box is working properly.
If you are not clear, consult the software developer or read the instructions carefully.
4.
Verify that AP isolation and guest mode are disabled on the router, and try to restart the router after adjustment.
The use of signal amplifiers or mobile carrier routing may also cause the device to not be found.
5.
iOS 14 and iPadOS 14 users can go to "Settings - Privacy - Local Network - TV Cast" to confirm that local network permissions are turned on.
6.
Turn off what your TV/box is playing and try casting the screen again.
7.
After the above confirmation is correct, you can restart the App and try the screen casting again.
8.
